Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Flow iQ?

Simply download the app, create an account, and follow the onboarding process. You'll be asked to enter some basic information about your cycle to help our AI provide accurate predictions.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. Your health data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties. You have complete control over your data and can export or delete it at any time.

How accurate are the AI predictions?

Flow iQ uses advanced machine learning algorithms with ensemble models achieving 95%+ accuracy. Predictions improve over time as the AI learns your unique patterns. All predictions include confidence scores so you know how reliable they are.

Do I need to grant HealthKit permissions?

HealthKit integration is optional. While it enhances biometric tracking features, all core functionality works without it. You can enable or disable HealthKit access anytime in Settings.

What languages does Flow iQ support?

Flow iQ is available in 36 languages, with full localization for all features. The app automatically uses your device's language, or you can change it in Settings.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export all your data in multiple formats (PDF, CSV) from the Settings menu. This is useful for sharing with healthcare providers or keeping personal records.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers. You can also choose to just clear your local data while keeping your account active.
