Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 23, 2025

Flow Ai ("we", "us", "our") is an AI‑powered health companion focused on privacy and security. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account Data: email address and hashed identifier.
  • Health & Tracking Data: cycle dates, symptoms, notes, and optional biometric data if you grant HealthKit/Google Fit permissions.
  • Device & Diagnostics: crash logs and product interaction for app reliability.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core app functionality and personalized AI predictions.
  • Sync your data across your devices if you enable cloud backup.
  • Improve reliability and performance via anonymized analytics.

What We Do Not Do

  • No selling of personal data.
  • No third‑party advertising or tracking across apps/websites.
  • No sharing of health data with third parties without your explicit consent.

HealthKit & Health Data Integration

Flow Ai integrates with Apple HealthKit (iOS) and Google Fit (Android) to provide enhanced menstrual health insights.

Data We Access from HealthKit/Google Fit:

  • Heart Rate & Heart Rate Variability (HRV): Used to correlate cardiovascular patterns with your menstrual cycle phases.
  • Body Temperature: Used for basal body temperature tracking and ovulation prediction.
  • Sleep Data: Analyzed to detect sleep pattern changes across your cycle.
  • Activity & Steps: Used to understand energy levels and wellness patterns.
  • Menstrual Flow Data: If you track flow in HealthKit, we can sync this data for unified tracking.
  • Resting Heart Rate & Respiratory Rate: Additional biometric markers for stress and recovery analysis.

How We Use Health Data:

  • Provide personalized cycle predictions and pattern recognition.
  • Generate AI-powered health insights specific to your menstrual health.
  • Detect potential irregularities or health patterns requiring attention.
  • Correlate lifestyle factors (sleep, activity) with cycle symptoms.

Your Control:

HealthKit/Google Fit access is completely optional. The app functions without these permissions, using only manually tracked data. You can:

  • Enable or disable permissions at any time in device Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Flow Ai.
  • Choose which specific health data types to share.
  • Revoke access without losing your manually entered data.

Data Sharing Promise: Health data from HealthKit/Google Fit is stored securely on your device and our encrypted servers. We never sell, rent, or share your health data with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry‑standard algorithms (e.g., TLS, AES). Access is restricted and logged.

Your Rights

  • Export your data from the app.
  • Delete your data and account at any time.
  • Withdraw permissions and opt out of analytics.

Children

Flow Ai is intended for users aged 13+ (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children under applicable age limits.

International Transfers

Data may be processed in the country where you live or in other countries with appropriate safeguards.
