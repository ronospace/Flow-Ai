## 📤 Upload via Apple Transporter

### Step 1: Open Apple Transporter
1. Launch **Apple Transporter** app
   - If not installed, download from [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/transporter/id1450874784)

### Step 2: Drag and Drop IPA
1. Drag `Flow Ai.ipa` from Finder into Apple Transporter
   - The file is located at: `build/ios/ipa/Flow Ai.ipa`
   - Finder window is already open to this location

### Step 3: Sign In
1. Sign in with your **App Store Connect** credentials
   - Use your Apple ID associated with your developer account

### Step 4: Deliver
1. Click the **"Deliver"** button
2. Wait for upload to complete (usually 5-15 minutes)
3. Check status in App Store Connect
