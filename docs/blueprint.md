# Flow Ai — Master Application Blueprint
## The Women’s Life, Wellness & Intelligence OS  
**By ZyraFlow GmbH™**

Flow Ai is the flagship consumer application of ZyraFlow GmbH™, designed as an AI-native, privacy-first super-app that supports every dimension of a woman’s daily life — body, mind, relationships, ambition, and wellbeing — within one unified ecosystem.

Flow Ai is not a tracker.  
It is a Life Operating System (Life OS) built to grow with a woman across decades.

---

## Core Design Principles
- Cycle-aware by default
- Emotionally intelligent UX
- Privacy and consent first
- Non-addictive, non-toxic engagement
- Culturally adaptive
- Long-term companionship (years, not sessions)
- Supportive and reflective, never prescriptive

---

## Hard Boundaries & Guardrails (Non-negotiable)
Flow Ai must never:
- Diagnose medical conditions
- Predict pregnancy outcomes
- Monitor fetal health metrics
- Replace clinicians, midwives, or therapists
- Provide treatment recommendations
- Issue medical risk scores or alerts
- Offer medical reassurance or alarm

Flow Ai is allowed to:
- Reflect patterns relative to personal baseline
- Normalize emotional and bodily experiences
- Encourage conversations with professionals
- Provide educational, non-directive information
- Support self-awareness and informed decision-making

Language enforcement:

Allowed phrasing:
- “You may notice…”
- “Compared to your usual pattern…”
- “Many women experience…”
- “Consider discussing this with a professional…”

Prohibited phrasing:
- “This means…”
- “You are at risk…”
- “Your baby is…”
- “You should…”
