Flow AI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2, 2024

Last Updated: September 2, 2024

1. Introduction

Flow AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Flow AI (the "App"). This App is designed to help users track their menstrual cycle, health symptoms, and receive AI-powered insights.

Important: We understand that menstrual health data is highly sensitive personal information. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and privacy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health Data

  • Menstrual cycle information (dates, flow intensity, symptoms)
  • Health symptoms and mood tracking
  • Personal notes and observations
  • Medication and contraceptive information (if provided)

2.2 Personal Information

  • Age and birth year (for cycle predictions)
  • Email address (for account creation and support)
  • Display name or username (optional)

2.3 Device and Usage Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and error logs (anonymized)
  • Analytics data for app improvement

2.4 Advertising Data

  • Advertising ID for personalized ads (if ads are enabled)
  • Ad interaction data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Cycle Predictions: AI algorithms analyze your data to provide personalized cycle predictions
  • Health Insights: Generate personalized health insights and recommendations
  • App Functionality: Enable core features like tracking, reminders, and data visualization
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and performance
  • Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your sensitive health data is primarily stored locally on your device using encryption. This means your most personal information remains under your direct control.

4.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud backup, your encrypted data may be stored on secure cloud servers to allow synchronization across devices. This is always optional and user-controlled.

4.3 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive health data
  • Biometric authentication options (fingerprint, Face ID)
  • Secure data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Health Data

Your menstrual cycle and health data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties for commercial purposes.

5.2 Limited Data Sharing

We may share non-health data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Anonymized analytics data with trusted service providers (Google Analytics, crash reporting)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with explicit user consent)

5.3 Advertising

We may display general health and wellness advertisements. Advertisers do not receive access to your personal health data. Ad targeting is based on general app category, not your specific health information.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your stored information within the app
  • Edit or Delete: Modify or delete any of your personal information
  • Export Data: Download your data in a portable format
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data

6.2 Privacy Controls

  • Disable analytics data collection
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Control cloud backup settings
  • Manage notification preferences

7. Children's Privacy

Flow AI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

Target Audience: This app is designed for individuals who menstruate, typically ages 13+ with parental guidance for teens.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for EU users.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience. These may include:

  • Essential cookies for app functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand app usage
  • Advertising cookies for relevant ads (if applicable)

You can control cookie preferences through your device settings.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings. Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for longer periods for app improvement purposes.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Analytics: For app usage analytics
  • Cloud Storage Providers: For optional data backup

Each service has its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.
